Union membership

It’s time to pay the mandatory fee for the spring term (VT25) to Jönköping Student Union. If you have already paid in the past few days or are a tuition paying student, you can disregard this email.

How do I pay?

The union fee is paid through the hitract app:
  1. Download the hitract app from AppStore or Google Play
  2. Create an account
  3. Search for “JSU” or “Jönköping Student Union”
  4. Click on “Become member”
  5. Choose the membership applicable to you, enter your personal number to verify through Ladok and pay the membership fee

Where can I find more information or support?

Please refer to the FAQ on our website: Hitract FAQ - Jönköpings Studentkår

What do I get from being a union member?
  • Join 9 000 other members, backing you when you want your voice heard
  • Enable events such as Kick Off, Valborg, Festive Friday, and much more.
  • Gain access to student discounts

... and much, much more.
